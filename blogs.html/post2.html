Top 10 Full-Body Exercises for Strength and Endurance

Your ultimate guide to building a stronger, fitter body.

1. Squats

Squats engage your legs, core, and back, making them a perfect full-body exercise for strength.

2. Deadlifts

One of the best exercises to strengthen your lower back, legs, and grip.

3. Push-Ups

A classic bodyweight exercise that works your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

4. Pull-Ups

Great for building upper-body strength, targeting your back, biceps, and core.

5. Burpees

A high-intensity movement that combines strength and endurance training.

6. Kettlebell Swings

Engages your hips, glutes, shoulders, and core while improving cardiovascular fitness.

7. Lunges

Builds leg and core strength while improving balance and stability.

8. Mountain Climbers

A full-body cardio workout that engages your arms, core, and legs.

9. Planks

Enhances core strength and endurance, improving posture and balance.

10. Jump Rope

A great way to improve coordination, endurance, and lower-body strength.
